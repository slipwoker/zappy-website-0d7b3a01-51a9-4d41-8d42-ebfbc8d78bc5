Our company

laryngo pro is a sister concern company of vision medical divices india PVT .LTD
We are Vision Medical Devices (India) Private Limited

Established in the year 2004, Vision Medical Devices India Private Limited is well established manufacturing company specialized in developing Laryngoscope (All type)as per the requirement of medical fraternity (Anaesthetist in Particular). Our Range of Laryngoscopes are constantly upgraded as per feedback from the user to provide them best Laryngoscopes making their lives easier. Our own intensive state- of- art manufacturing unit has helped us maintain the quality of Laryngoscopes and other products with accessories/raw material used/outsourced from the world's best companies. This has helped us attaining prestigious quality certifications including ISO 13485:2016 from BSI UK and CE Confirming to EU declaration. All This has been achieved due to efforts of the company Managing Director Mr. Avi Bhonkar who has tremendous 29 years of past experience of establishing manufacturing unit of Truphatek in India independently

Advantages

Our Laryngoscopes are well accepted and use in major hospitals in Mumbai, Western & Southern India through our dealers & Distributors. Our Laryngoscopes are approved by Anaesthetist, Intensivists & Bio-Medical Engineers and made available in respective hospitals after complete quality checks conducted by them on their patients .Vision Laryngoscopes are as per ISO standards & anatomically designed as per the need of Anesthetics.
